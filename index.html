About Me

My Journey

From a B.Tech in IT at Vishnu Institute of Technology (2017–2023) to discovering my passion for design: I transitioned into UX/UI through the Google UX Design Professional Certificate and Internshala’s UI/UX Design program. Since then I've delivered several web and mobile projects spanning ecommerce, social, entertainment and fintech domains.

Values & Strengths

Empathy‑driven and detail‑oriented, I love simplifying complex problems into clear user flows. I thrive in collaborative teams and believe that inclusive, accessible design makes better products for everyone.

Tools & Expertise

Figma & FigJam, Adobe Photoshop. End‑to‑end UX: research, user interviews, journey mapping, wireframing & flows, high‑fidelity UIs and prototyping. Skilled in building responsive design systems and iterating based on user feedback.
